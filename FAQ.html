Frequently Asked Questions

Where do the lessons take place?

I teach Online and in person. I'm based in Newport Wales and willing to travel as far as Cardiff and Bristol.

What times do you teach?

Usually between 10.00am to 8.00pm from Monday to Friday. Could also do weekends if that suits you better.

How often should I have gutiar lessons?

I recomend once a week as that gives you enough time to practise and not enought time to forget what we've done during the previous lesson.

Do I need a guitar to start?

Ideally yes, however if you don't own one I could let you use one of mine for your first lesson (if in person). I could also help you choose your first guitar!

How do I book a lesson?

You just need to contact me. Either give me a ring or drop a message. I try to reply I fast as I can. We can then arrange a place and time that suits you.

How much do the lessons cost?

Your first lesson is FREE! After that it's £20 per 30-minute session & £30 per 1-hour session.
