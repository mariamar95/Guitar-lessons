About the Lessons...

My goal is not only to teach you how to play like your hands are on fire, but to find a style that suits you, whilst teaching you how to be a rockstar. That’s right, all the parts of musical education that your secondary school music teacher forgot to mention like; how to secure a guitar to your back, how to use and select the right guitar pedals, wireless guitar frequencies, and all the other good stuff, and tricks of the trade, that a professional needs to know to perform comfortably on stage. This way you can focus on what you were born to do, which is entertaining the audience. My lessons will provide you with a solid mix of musical theory, performance, but also practical and hardware lessons to teach you not just about music, but about the instrument and all the things that go along with it.
